Random Number Generator

Generate a number between 0 and 255. Why that range? Only cool people understand the reference.

Aug 22, 2022 • 1 min read 

Howdy there! I'm Python. I can do lots of cool stuff.
Before I can generate a random number for you, you have to give me permission to do so. May I generate a number for you?
Okay! Your number is... 86!
Now, I can't actually see what number I just gave you. I can generate another one, and there's a one in 256 chance that it's the same. I'll tell you a secret if it is.
Do you want me to generate a new one?
Cool! And my number is...
141!
Welp! Looks like my secret is safe for now...
